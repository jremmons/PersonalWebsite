Visit to the Laboratory for Preclinical Imaging and Imaging Technology of the Werner Siemens-Foundat
 

Host: Mrs.  Fischer
Summary: The student learned about the first PET-MRI machine and the ongoing developments of single-photon avalanche diode, also know as G-APDs, for their suitability to be used in high resolution PET applications and under the exposure to high magnetic fields for combined PET/MRI studies. Clinical studies with small animals were presented. The students visited the research facilities and the electronic laboratory
